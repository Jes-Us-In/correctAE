CorrectAE

CorrectAE is an application designed and developed to automate the process of correcting and evaluating multiple-choice exams. It belongs to the OMR (Optical Mark Recognition) family of tools. Its main characteristic feature is that, instead of using specialiced reading machines and specific answer sheets, as this type of application does, its operation is based on the use of ordinary paper sheets for answers, A4 size. The image on this sheets can be captured through an ordinary commercial image scanner. With the graphic files obtained, CorrectAE is fed for data capture and evaluation.
